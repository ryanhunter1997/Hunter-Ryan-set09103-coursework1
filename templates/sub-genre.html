Sub Genres

Metal

Metal is a genre of rock music that developed in the late 1960s and early 1970s, largely in the UK. With roots in blues and psychedelic rock, the bands that created metal developed a thick, massive sound, charactized by highly amplified distortion, extended guitar solos, emphatic beats, and overall loudness.







Indie

Indie rock is a genre of alternative rock that orginiated in the United States and the UK in the 1980s. Orginally "Indie" was being independent from a record label and taking a do-it-yourself approach to publishing and recording music. During the 1990s Britpop bands in the UK broke into the mainstream









Alternative

Alternative rock is a style of rock music that emerged from the independent music underground of the 1980s and became popular in the 1990s. At times alt rock has been used as an umbrella description for undergorund rock music.
